Post-treatment follow-up appointment to monitor healing and progress.

HOW TO PREPARE FOR YOUR APPOINTMENT
====================================
- Brush and floss your teeth before arriving.
- Arrive 10 minutes early to complete any paperwork if it's your first visit.
- Bring a list of any medications you are currently taking.
- If you have dental anxiety, let us know ahead of time so we can accommodate you.
- Avoid eating or drinking anything (other than water) 30 minutes before your appointment.

CANCELLATION POLICY
===================
Appointments can be cancelled up to 24 hours in advance.
After that period, a 40% fee of the appointment cost will be charged.
